Curry

Description

So Easy, Anyone Can Make It.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • Some chicken
  • Some potatoes
  • Some carrots
  • Milk and curry blocks
  • Salt (if needed)
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Start by heating oil in a pan. Add onion, stir-fry until fragrant.
  2. Add potatoes and carrots, and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Start cooking rice in a separate pot to save time.
  4. Add the chicken to the pan (you can marinate it beforehand with seasonings, but if youâ€™re lazy, you can skip this step).
  5. Once everything in the pan is cooked, pour in milk and add the curry blocks.
  6. Simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring constantly to prevent sticking.
  7. Finally, serve and plate the dish nicely.

Adjust quantities and taste to your preference.
